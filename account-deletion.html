Delete Your Account & Data
Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how workers ("you") using the GoHelp Partner app can request deletion of your account and the personal data associated with it, and what happens to that data afterwards. This applies to the GoHelp Partner (maid/cook) app only — see the customer app's privacy policy for that app's data practices.

🔒 Deleting your account is permanent. Once processed, you will not be able to log back in with the same phone number, and this cannot be undone.

1. Delete From Inside the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account:

  1. Open the GoHelp Partner app and log in
  2. Go to Profile (bottom tab)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account, below the Logout button
  4. Confirm when asked — your account is deactivated and your sensitive data (Aadhaar, PAN, selfie, bank details, home address, emergency contact) is cleared immediately

2. No Access to the App?

If you can't open the app anymore, email us at 100rblakhmara@gmail.com from any address, with your registered phone number and the subject line "Delete my GoHelp Partner account". We'll process it manually within 7 business days and confirm by email or SMS once done.
