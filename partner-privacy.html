Privacy Policy — GoHelp Partner
Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the GoHelp Partner app — the companion app used by maids and cooks ("workers", "you") who provide services through GoHelp. It is separate from, and describes different data practices than, the GoHelp customer app. GoHelp ("we", "us", "our") explains below how we collect, use, and protect your personal information as a worker on our platform.

🔒 We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

To onboard and pay you correctly, we collect:

  • Phone number — for OTP-based login and account identification
  • Name, date of birth, gender — for your worker profile
  • Home address & pincode — to assign you to a service zone near you
  • Emergency contact name, relation & phone number — for your safety
  • Identity documents — photos of your Aadhaar card (front & back), PAN card, and a selfie — used only to verify your identity before approval
  • Bank account details — account holder name, account number, IFSC code — used only to pay your weekly salary
  • Location (GPS) — collected only while you mark attendance or have an active customer visit in progress
  • Push notification token — to send you approval, job-assignment and other app alerts
  • Preferred language — to show the app in Hindi, Bengali or English as you choose

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and review your application to join GoHelp
  • To assign you to a service zone and unlock your daily work slots after attendance
  • To track visit progress (arrival geofencing) so customers know when you're on the way
  • To calculate and transfer your weekly salary to your bank account
  • To notify you about approval status, new job assignments, and important updates
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Customers — see only your first name, profile photo, and rating. Your phone number, home address, and identity documents are never shared with customers
  • Bank transfer — your bank details are used solely to deposit your salary and are never shared with customers or third parties
  • Identity documents — used only for internal verification by our admin team; never sold, shared, or shown to customers
  • SMS provider — your phone number is shared with MSG91 to send OTP verification codes
  • Push notifications — a device token is shared with Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver app notifications
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law

4. Location Data

Location is collected only in the foreground — while you actively mark attendance or have an in-progress visit open in the app. We do not track your location in the background or outside of active work sessions.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data, including identity document photos, is stored on secure servers via Supabase (PostgreSQL and encrypted file storage). We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Access to worker data is restricted to authorised personnel only. We do not store your Aadhaar number as text — only the document photos you upload for verification.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active, and attendance/visit history for legal and payroll purposes. You can request full deletion of your account and data at any time (see Contact section below).

7. Age Requirement

GoHelp Partner is intended for workers aged 18–60. We do not knowingly onboard anyone outside this age range. If you believe this has happened in error, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location access via your device settings

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • MSG91 — SMS / OTP delivery (msg91.com/privacy-policy)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  • Supabase — secure database & document storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery platform (expo.dev/privacy)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
