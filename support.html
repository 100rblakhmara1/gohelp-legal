Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a home cleaning service?

Open the GoHelp app → tap a service on the home screen → choose a date and time slot → confirm your address → complete payment. You'll receive a booking confirmation immediately.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to My Bookings in the app → tap the booking → scroll down and tap Cancel Booking. Cancellations follow our refund policy above.

My worker hasn't arrived — what do I do?

If your worker is more than 15 minutes late, please email us at 100rblakhmara@gmail.com with your booking ID. We'll contact the worker immediately and keep you updated.

I'm not happy with the quality of service.

We're sorry to hear that. Please email us within 24 hours of the completed service with your booking ID and details. We take quality seriously and will arrange a free re-clean or issue a refund.

My payment failed but amount was deducted.

Failed payment amounts are automatically refunded by Razorpay within 5–7 business days. If you don't see the refund after 7 days, email us with your phone number and approximate amount — we'll check with Razorpay directly.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at 100rblakhmara@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and your registered phone number. We'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.

Which cities does GoHelp serve?

GoHelp is currently available in select cities across India. The app automatically detects whether your location is serviceable when you enter your address. We're expanding rapidly — if your city isn't available yet, you can join the waitlist in the app.

Is GoHelp available on iOS and Android?

Yes. GoHelp is available on both the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). Search for "GoHelp – Home Cleaning Service".
